DEPRESSION TREATEMENT
  1. Diagnosis of depression is crucial in order to expedite the healing process.

  2. Often wrong medication and counselling leads a person experiencing depression to sink further into adverse conditions.

  3. Getting professional help from a doctor and a mental health expert is the right way to start.

  4. Treatments shall be initiated depending upon the type and condition of depression that you are being diagnosed with.

  5. It’s important to remember that involving yourself in ‘talk therapy‘ with your counsellor, letting him or her cross the threshold of your emotional resistance is crucial towards the healing process.

ANXIETY TREATEMENT
  1. It’s normal to feel anxious about moving to a new place, starting a new job, or taking a test.

  2. In the case of an anxiety disorder, the feeling of fear may be with you all the time.

  3. This type of anxiety may cause you to stop doing things you enjoy.

  4. Anxiety disorders are the most common form of emotional disorder and can affect anyone at any age.

  5. women are more likely than men to be diagnosed with an anxiety disorder.

BIPOLAR DISORDER TREATEMENT
  1. Bipolar disorder is a lifelong condition that runs an unpredictable course of ups and downs.

  2. When left untreated, these ups and downs can be devastating.

  3. The recurring manic and depressive episodes that characterize the disease make it difficult to lead a stable, productivelife.

  4. In the manic phase, you may be hyperactive and irresponsible. In the depressive phase, it may be difficult to do anything at all.

  5. Recovering from bipolar disorder doesn’t happen overnight.
